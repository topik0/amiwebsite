Ami Commands / Moderation

.purge.purge {number of messages}Bulk delete a certain amount of messages.
.ban.ban {user}Banish a user from your server.
.kick.kick {user}Kick a user from your server.
.mute.mute {user}Mute a user in your server.
.hackban.hackban {user ID}Ban a user who is not in the server.
.unmute.unmute {number of messages}Unmute a currently muted user.
.tempban.tempban {number of messages}Temporarily ban a user.
.voicekick.voicekick {number of messages}Kick a user from a voice channel.
.unban.unban {number of messages}Unban a banned user from your server.
.tempmute.tempmute {number of messages}Temporarily mute a user.
.softban.softban {number of messages}Ban a user and unban them to get rid of their sent messages.
